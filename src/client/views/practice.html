Perfect Your Facial Punctuation.

Public Speaking

eNalysis gives you the tools to perfect your facial punctuation in any speech you give. Seeing the visual data allows you to structure your expressions the way you want and allowing yourself to emphasize your delivery at the right points.

Interviews

In any interview session, if you are delivering or receiving it is best to know what emotion you are showing on your face. eNalysis can help with your confidence and inform you how much eye contact you are making and if you are showing confidence.

Acting

All actors need to show emotion, whether it very suble or obvious. eNalysis can deconstruct the facial expressions and show graphically how dramatic your emotions are. With our practice mode, it can get you that much closer to Hollywood.
